## Full Description (max 4000 chars)

Indian Army Tactical is a premium digital watch face for Wear OS inspired by Indian Army field-issue tactical timepieces. Featuring authentic 7-segment LCD digits, military-grade layout, and full customization.

FEATURES

- 7-Segment LCD Display — Authentic DSEG7 digital font with ghost segment effect for that real LCD look
- Live Complications — Battery percentage with segmented progress bar, heart rate (BPM), and daily step count
- Weather Complication — Configurable weather/info slot in the sub-display
- Day of Year (DOY) — Military-standard day-of-year counter
- Indian Tricolor — Saffron, white, and green accent lines honoring the Indian flag
- 12H/24H Auto-Detection — Automatically follows your device time format with AM/PM or 24H indicator
- Show/Hide Seconds — Toggle seconds display on or off to save battery

CUSTOMIZATION

5 preset themes in the watch face picker:
- Tactical Amber — Classic military LCD orange on dark green
- Tactical Green — Stealth green on dark green
- Stealth Black — Monochrome gray on pure black
- Urban Blue — Cool blue on dark gray
- Red Alert — Urgent red on dark green

Additional configuration via the watch face editor:
- Accent Color — 5 color options (amber, green, blue, red, stealth gray)
- Background Theme — 3 background options (tactical, stealth, urban)
- Show Seconds — Toggle on/off
- Weather Slot — Tap to configure with any SHORT_TEXT complication

DESIGN DETAILS

- Dual bezel rings with 12 tactical tick marks
- Sub-display panel with date, day-of-year, and weather
- Three bottom info panels (BATT, HR, STEPS) with dark tactical styling
- "=== TACTICAL ===" footer branding
- Full ambient mode support with monochrome HH:MM display

TECHNICAL

- Watch Face Format v2 (WFF) — lightweight, resource-only, no background code
- Optimized for Wear OS 4+ (API 34+)
- Under 110 KB installed size
- No phone companion required — fully standalone
- No permissions needed — no data collection

Designed with pride for the soldiers and veterans of the Indian Armed Forces.

## Upload to Play Console

1. Go to https://play.google.com/console
2. Select your app → Store presence → Main store listing
3. Upload assets:
   - App icon: app_icon_512.png
   - Feature graphic: feature_graphic_1024x500.png
   - Screenshots: screenshot_main.png + screenshot_picker_final.png (min 2 required)
4. Fill in Short description and Full description from above
5. Set Content rating → Everyone
6. Under Release → Production → upload app-release.aab
